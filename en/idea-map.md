Date: 2026-04-18

epistemic agents, civilizational steering, and continuity.

## Conceptual Summary

Together these essays describe a larger stack:

- maintain uncertainty instead of collapsing it too early,
- interpret signals through multiple epistemic agents,
- steer civilization across scenario distributions,
- preserve knowledge and recovery capacity if steering fails.
